Design a complete SaaS-style agency operating system interface for a digital marketing agency called Kind Supply Digital.

The platform should look like a hybrid between Notion, HubSpot, ClickUp, and Stripe dashboards, designed to manage a multi-million-dollar digital marketing agency handling dozens of clients simultaneously.

The design should be extremely structured, professional, minimal, and scalable, with clear hierarchy and modern UI components.

Color palette should feel premium, minimal, and technology-forward.

The system must include the following dashboards and modules.

GLOBAL AGENCY DASHBOARD

Create the main command center dashboard.

This dashboard shows a real-time overview of the entire agency.

Widgets should include:

Total Monthly Recurring Revenue
Active Clients
Active Campaigns
Website Projects
Leads Generated This Month
Ad Spend Managed
Agency Profit Margin
Upcoming Client Deliverables
Team Workload Distribution
Client Health Indicators

Include charts:
