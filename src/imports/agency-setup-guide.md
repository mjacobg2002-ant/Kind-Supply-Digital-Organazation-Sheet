1. Core Client Infrastructure (Absolute Essentials)

These are the non-negotiable tools you should have ready.

CRM (to track leads you call)

You need somewhere to track:

• leads
• calls
• follow-ups
• deal stage

Best options:

Notion (since you’re building your OS already)

HubSpot CRM (very good free CRM)

Your CRM should track:

Lead name
Company
Phone
Email
Industry
Call status
Next follow-up
Potential monthly value

Payment Processing

Once someone says yes, you need to take payment immediately.

Use:

Stripe

Set up:

• monthly subscription ($4,500)
• ad management fee ($1,500)
• one-time website build ($8,000)

Create payment links so you can send instantly.

Contracts

You must have a service agreement ready.

Use:

DocuSign
or

PandaDoc

Contract should include:

Scope of services
Monthly retainer
Ad spend responsibility
Cancellation clause
Payment terms

Client Communication

Choose one main communication platform.

Best options:

Slack

Google Workspace (email + calendar)

For starting out:

Email + Google Meet is perfectly fine.
